About Valo

Software for the work that matters most

Valo exists for the organisations that hold funds on behalf of others and turn them into real outcomes for real people. We bring identity, payments, data and reporting together in one trusted platform, so the people doing this work can spend less time fighting systems and more time making a difference.

Why we exist

The systems that hold trust were never built for it

Organisations that manage funds for others have been stitching together spreadsheets, finance tools and case systems that were never designed to talk to each other. The result is duplicated effort, slow reporting and money that is hard to trace back to the people it was meant for.

We started Valo to fix that. One platform where identity, money, data and outcomes finally live together, built specifically for the high-trust, high-accountability world of social investment.

What we believe

The principles we build by

These are the ideas that shape every product decision we make, from the smallest setting to the shape of the whole platform.

Trust is the whole job

Not bolted on afterwards. The controls that protect your funds and your data are part of the platform itself.

People before process

Behind every payment and every record is a person being supported. We build for the coordinators, carers and finance teams who make that support real.

Outcomes, not just outputs

It is not enough to move money. We help organisations show the difference that money made, so good work can be seen, funded and repeated.

Open by default

The product and the platform are the same thing. That means no feature is ever locked away from your own systems.

Configurable, not rigid

No two organisations work the same way. Valo bends to fit how you actually operate, instead of forcing you to change to suit the software.

Built to last

Cloud-native, secure and always improving. We are building infrastructure that organisations can rely on for the long haul.
